App Development

Build cross-platform mobile apps with Flutter, React Native, and Kotlin.

Introduction

App Development is the process of creating software applications that run on mobile devices — smartphones and tablets. With over 6 billion smartphone users worldwide, mobile apps have become the primary way people interact with digital services. From social media and banking to health tracking and e-commerce, mobile apps are an essential part of modern life and a massive industry worth hundreds of billions of dollars.

Our App Development program teaches you to build apps for both Android and iOS using the most popular cross-platform frameworks.

What is App Development?

Mobile app development is the act or process by which a mobile app is developed for mobile devices, such as personal digital assistants, enterprise digital assistants, or mobile phones. These applications can be pre-installed on phones, downloaded from mobile app stores, or accessed via mobile web browsers. App development requires knowledge of programming languages, UI/UX design, device hardware access, and platform-specific guidelines.

"The best apps are the ones that solve a real problem in the simplest possible way — and feel natural on the device."

History & Significance

Mobile app development began with the launch of the iPhone in 2007 and the App Store in 2008. Android followed with its own marketplace in 2008. The rise of smartphones created an entirely new industry, with millions of apps available today. Cross-platform frameworks like React Native (2015) and Flutter (2017) revolutionized development by allowing code sharing across platforms. The mobile app market is projected to reach over $600 billion by 2030, making app development one of the most lucrative career paths in tech.
